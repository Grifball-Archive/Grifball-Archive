# Historical record

This section holds **chronology and context**: when major formats appeared, how communities overlapped, and which sources support each claim.

## Why a separate “historical record”

Articles can tell a story in depth; statistics can hold raw numbers. The **historical record** pages tie those together at a glance: a season overview might link to an article about finals night, a CSV of standings, and a playlist of VODs. Readers browsing years later should be able to answer “what happened, roughly when, and where can I read more?” without opening Discord.

## What to add here

- **Overview pages** for each era, league, or game title you care about.
- **Timelines** (tables or lists) with dates and short descriptions; link out to articles and stats pages.
- **Source notes** (links, screenshots of primary posts, archived pages) so future editors can verify entries.
